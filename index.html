Automatic Remote Watering System

We made an easy-to-build and cheap automatic watering system, one that can be controlled through your phone and automatically activate depending on enviornmental conditions. Make your garden water-efficient and get current information anywhere.

IOT Irrigation system

We engineered a way to completely remotely and automatically control your garden. At any time, anywhere, you have insight into the conditions of your enviornment. You can control automatic watering, at intervals or at moisture levels, giving you precise control with ease.

Works anywhere. Cheap to build.

Implementation

Extremely easy to implement, all it needs is an internet connection and a connection to solenoid valves.

Sizing

Our V1 model measures at 14" x 12" x 2"
This size can be reduced in all directions to fit in any space.

Materials

For a garden setting, plastic is likely the best route: cheap, accessible, and extremely durable and waterproof.

The cost per unit is accessable, and can be done effectively for under 50 dollars with time and effort.

Open Source

Everything from the materials, software, and displays can change case by case. 
One can easily change how the device functions, from a easy-to-access database or through chip software.

Instant data

Constantly updated:
- Soil moisture (water %)
- Previous watering time
- Lighting information
- Internet connectivity 

All on your phone.

Automatic

Using a well-designed framework, the thresholds for automatic watering are easily controllable, anywhere, at any time, instantly.

Our build

Our current system, the first version, works greatly. We are looking to improve production costs, speed, and looks.

How it works

Our system is a two-way communication device, connecting the device to send constant information and updates through Wi-Fi, all to a database that is unique to your device. With this constant updating, you can change many specifics about watering patterns and conditions, and also have updates on conditions at all times, from anywhere.
